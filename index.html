      HyperSynergy CDSS: Official User Guide

      Welcome to the HyperSynergy Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS)! This website acts as an intelligent assistant for Traditional Medicine (TM) practitioners and researchers. It uses a powerful AI (Graph Neural Network) to read traditional herbal formulas and predict if the combination of herbs will be safe, balanced, and effective.

      You do not need to be a computer scientist or a master herbalist to use this tool. This guide will walk you through the interface and explain exactly how to interpret the AI's results.

      🖥️ 1. Understanding the Interface

      • The Left Panel (Build Prescription): This is your workspace. Here, you will search for herbs from our 714-herb database and combine them into a digital "formula."
      • The Top Right Panel (Dashboard): This is the AI's quick-glance summary. It gives you the overall Synergy Score, the Biological Distance, and a Formula Balance check.
      • The Bottom Right Panel (AI Role Assignments): This detailed table explains why the AI gave you that score. It breaks down every herb and reveals its specific job in the formula.

      🛠️ 2. Step-by-Step: How to Use the App

      Step 1: Search and Add Herbs

      • Go to the Build Prescription panel on the left.
      • Click the search bar and type an herb's name (e.g., Đương Quy or Đại táo).
      • Click the herb you want, or click the blue "+" button to add it to your "Current Formula" list.
      • Note: You must add at least two herbs to run an analysis.

      Step 2: Run the AI

      Once your herbs are listed, click the big blue "Run Live PyTorch Analysis" button. The website will send your formula to our cloud AI, performing millions of calculations in milliseconds.

      Step 3: Read the Executive Summary

      Look at the Executive Summary & Recommendation box. It will explicitly tell you if the formula is highly viable, structurally incomplete, or at a high risk of clinical conflict.

      📊 3. How to Understand the Results

      Overall Synergy Probability

      This is the ultimate safety and effectiveness score.

      • 80% - 100%: Excellent. Works perfectly together.
      • 50% - 79%: Moderate. Could be stronger.
      • Below 50%: Poor. High risk of side effects.

      Biological Distance (Poincaré Metric)

      The AI maps herbs in a 3D hyperbolic geometry.

      • Tight (Good): Herbs target similar diseases/pathways.
      • Far (Poor): Herbs pull the body in conflicting directions.

      Formula Balance Composition

      Ancient medicine relies on the Quân-Thần-Tá-Sứ rule. A safe formula must be balanced.

      • Main Healer (Architect): The formula must have an aggressive herb to fight the disease.
      • Protector (Buffer): The formula must have a gentle herb (like Honey or Đại táo) to protect the stomach from the harsh Main Healer.

      If the AI gives you a red "X" here, it means you forgot a crucial piece of the puzzle!

      The XAI Table (Role Assignments)

      XAI stands for "Explainable AI." The AI scores every herb's importance ($\alpha$ Weight) from 0.0 to 1.0. Based on that score, it automatically categorizes the herb:

      Architect (Sovereign): The boss. Directs the healing.
      Bridge (Minister): The transporter. Moves medicine to the right organ.
      Buffer (Assistant): The bodyguard. Protects the patient from side effects.

      Pro-Tip for your First Try!

      2. Xuyên khung (The Bridge - moves blood)
      3. Đại táo (The Buffer - protects the stomach)

      About This Project

      The HyperSynergy CDSS is the culmination of a Ph.D. research project focusing on Macro-to-Micro Geometric Learning. The underlying Graph Neural Network (MATG) is trained on the DoTatLoi-714 benchmark dataset, structuring centuries of Vietnamese Traditional Medicine (VTM) knowledge into a rigorous topological manifold.
